The Training and Placement Cell (TPC) is the back bone of VJIT. It provides a launch pad for students to make the best career for them in the fields of Software & IT, Core Industries (Mechanical, Electronics, Civil and Electrical), Management etc. in the form of Placements and Internships. Over the years, the Cell has maintained good and strong relationship with Industries across the country and as a result, has built up an impressive placement record both in terms of percentage of registered students placed, as well as the number of companies visiting the campus.
